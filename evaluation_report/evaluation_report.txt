INTERPRETATION GUIDE
======================================================================

• Pixel Accuracy: Overall percentage of correctly classified pixels
• IoU (Intersection over Union): Overlap between prediction and ground truth
• Dice Coefficient: F1-score variant for segmentation (2*TP / (2*TP + FP + FN))
• Precision: Ratio of true positives to all predicted positives
• Recall: Ratio of true positives to all actual positives
• F1-Score: Harmonic mean of precision and recall

Performance Thresholds:
  - Excellent: IoU > 0.7, Dice > 0.8
  - Good: IoU 0.5-0.7, Dice 0.65-0.8
  - Fair: IoU 0.3-0.5, Dice 0.45-0.65
  - Poor: IoU < 0.3, Dice < 0.45
